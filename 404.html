How to play

The goal is to memorise the order of shuffled playing cards. Can you memorise the whole deck?

♠ Choose between 1 and 52 cards to memorise and enter this value into the box.

♦ Click play now to lock in your choice and display the cards

♣ When you have memorised the cards, click on guess to hide the cards

♥ Test your memory by filling out the order of the cards in the table. Submit your answers to see if you are right!
